Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 27, 2026

1. Our Commitment to Your Privacy

PDF.web.id ("we", "us", "our"), accessible at pdf.web.id, is a privacy-focused PDF toolkit. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information when you use our service. Our fundamental principle is straightforward: your documents belong to you alone. We do not and cannot view, access, store, or share your files. Every PDF operation is performed entirely on your own device, within your web browser (client-side processing).

1.1 How Client-Side Processing Works

Unlike conventional online PDF services that require file uploads to remote servers, PDF.web.id operates differently. All tools are powered by JavaScript and WebAssembly libraries that execute directly within your browser. Your data never travels over the internet to any external server, ensuring the highest level of privacy and data security possible.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Due to our client-side architecture, it is technically impossible for us to collect the following:

  • The content of your PDF files or any other documents you process using our tools.
  • Any personal data embedded within your documents.
  • Filenames or metadata of your documents.
  • Any information derived from your files. All data used during your active session is immediately discarded when you close or navigate away from the page.

3. Information We May Collect (Non-Personal Data)

To enhance website performance and understand general usage patterns, we may gather anonymous, non-personally identifiable information. This data helps us determine which tools are most utilized and where improvements can be made. This includes:

  • Anonymous Usage Statistics: Aggregated data about tool usage frequency and feature access patterns. This information cannot be linked to any individual user or document.
  • Performance Metrics: Anonymized error reports and performance data used to identify and resolve technical issues. No personal information or file content is included.

We exclusively use privacy-respecting analytics solutions. We do not employ tracking cookies for advertising, retargeting, or cross-site profiling purposes.

4. Third-Party Libraries

PDF.web.id is built upon well-established, open-source libraries including PDF-lib.js, PDF.js, and various WebAssembly modules. These libraries are widely trusted by the developer community and operate under the same client-side processing principle. While we have reviewed these libraries, we encourage users to examine their respective documentation for additional details.

5. Data Security

Because your files are never transmitted to any server, you are inherently protected from server-side data breaches, unauthorized access, or data interception during transit. The security of your documents depends solely on the security of your own device and web browser.

6. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will promptly take steps to remove such information.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We recommend reviewing this policy from time to time to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.
